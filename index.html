The official home of Grandma's original recipe

INGREDIENTS
  • 3oz Philadelphia Cream Cheese
  • 1/4 cup Heinz Ketchup
  • 1/2 tsp dry mustard
  • 3/4 cup sour cream
  • Dash of onion salt

Allow cream cheese to soften. Blend all ingredients in bowl. Chill and serve.

Grandma recommends Lay's Classic potato chips.

MOM'S ALTERNATIVE VERSION

Substitute onion salt with one package Lipton Onion Soup mix. Add extra ketchup to taste.

Mom recommends Utz potato chips.
